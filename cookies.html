Cookies Policy
Last updated: 7th April, 2023.
This Cookies Policy explains how MOGERE PPE STORES ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and other similar technologies on our website. We encourage you to read this policy carefully so that you understand our use of cookies and can make informed choices about whether to accept or reject them.
What are cookies?
Cookies are small data files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. Cookies allow the website to remember your actions and preferences (such as login details, language, font size, and other display preferences) over a period of time. This makes your browsing experience more convenient and efficient when you revisit the same website.
How we use cookies
We use cookies on our Website for the following purposes:
Essential cookies: These cookies are necessary for the Website to function properly and cannot be switched off in our systems. They are usually set in response to actions made by you, such as setting your privacy preferences, logging in, or filling in forms. You can set your browser to block or alert you about these cookies, but some parts of the Website may not work if you do so.
Performance cookies: These cookies help us improve the performance and functionality of the Website. They allow us to measure and analyze traffic and user behavior on the Website, such as which pages are visited most frequently, how visitors navigate the Website, and how long they spend on each page. This information helps us to improve the design, content, and functionality of the Website.
Functional cookies: These cookies enable the Website to provide enhanced functionality and personalization. They may be set by us or by third-party providers whose services we have added to our pages. If you do not allow these cookies, then some or all of these services may not function properly.
Targeting cookies: These cookies are used to deliver personalized content and advertising to you based on your browsing behavior and interests. They are also used to limit the number of times you see an advertisement and to measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns. These cookies may be set by us or by third-party advertisers. We do not use cookies to collect or store personally identifiable information about you.
Third-party cookies
We may use third-party services on our Website that may set cookies on your device. These third-party services may include social media platforms, advertising networks, and analytics providers. We do not control the use of these cookies and cannot access them. You should check the relevant third-party website for more information about their use of cookies.
How to manage cookies
You can control and manage cookies in various ways, depending on your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:
Accept all cookies
Reject all cookies
Accept only certain types of cookies
Delete cookies when you close your browser
Block third-party cookies
Please note that if you disable or reject cookies, some parts of the Website may not work properly.
